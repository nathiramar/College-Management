Privacy Policy

VSN College of Engineering & Technology is committed to protecting the privacy of students, faculty, parents, and visitors. The information collected through this College Management System is used only for academic and administrative purposes.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as Name, Register Number, Department, Email Address, Phone Number, and other details required for college administration.

How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used to manage admissions, student records, faculty details, attendance, communication, and other educational services.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Confidentiality

Personal information will not be shared with third parties except when required by law or with the user's permission.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the college administration through the Contact page.
